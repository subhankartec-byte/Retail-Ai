Our Story

Retail AI began inside a real store — with a manager tired of losing evenings to manual Excel work. Stock validation, audit reconciliation, transfer planning: every routine task became a tool, and every tool was tested against live SOH data from day one.

Today those tools help retail teams turn raw SAP exports into decisions in seconds — with the polish of enterprise software and the practicality of something built by someone who has actually closed a store at 10 pm.
